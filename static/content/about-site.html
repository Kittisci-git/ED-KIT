About ED:KIT

ED:KIT is a browser-based toolkit built to support commanders across the galaxy in planning and managing their colonised systems. Whether you're shaping the future of a faction or mapping out your first outpost, ED:KIT helps turn complex spreadsheets into simple, easy-to-use plans.


The site was created by Cmdr Kittisci, a long-time player with a deep interest in colony-building and system development. What began as a set of personal tools for managing a long-term colonisation effort gradually evolved into a fully-fledged planner designed to be shared. ED:KIT is built with for commanders who enjoy the creative process of system planning and want a clear way to visualise and control every aspect of their projects.


While the Colony Planner is the first tool available, ED:KIT is intended to grow. Throughout development, the guiding principle has been simple: to support the Elite Dangerous community by providing thoughtful, usable tools that make deep gameplay more accessible and satisfying.


ED:KIT is not affiliated with Frontier Developments and is offered freely as a community resource.
